My learning plan

My future goal is to have a job as a web developer hopefully doing something for Te ao Maori. I would love to have that location freedom to work remotely or overseas if I wanted to as well.

I want to work on being more organised with my time. This week was stressful for me as I started mid week so I put that unnecessary pressure on myself. I will work on my time management in the future by starting things at a reasonable time and using timer apps.

I am going to manage my workload by taking more breaks. I am finding I get more fatigued when I don't take regular breaks to give my eyes a rest, and I am much more productive after having had one.

I am committed to trying to figure out the problems on my own first. I have found on a few occasions I have jumped the gun, asked for help, tried to figure it out on my own whilst waiting on a response from someone. And funnily enough I end up working it out on my own sometimes… I think that falls from the lack of confidence weakness that I mentioned in my identity and values section.

My expectations of the facillitators is that they will be available, supportive and compassionate, I Will be seeking for help through Youtube, discord channels and google. I currently have no block out times that will effect my learning in the future.
