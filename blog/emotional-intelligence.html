EQ and IQ

IQ tests measure your ability to solve problems, use logic, and grasp or communicate complex ideas. EQ tests measure your ability to recognise emotion in yourself and others, and to use that awareness to guide your decisions

Emotional intelligence to me is understanding and being self aware of your feelings and how to control the emotions of yourself and others around you. I believe having a higher EQ allows you to understand others, show initiative and gives you the ability to work well in a team and show compassion.

Having a high IQ is equally as important as EQ as it gives you the ability to think logically. They both compliment eachother however if you are lacking in emotional intelligence I think you will find it hard to relate to others and build stable relationships.
